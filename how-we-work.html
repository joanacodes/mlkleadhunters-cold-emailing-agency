Targeting

The right 300 prospects beat the wrong 3,000

Most outreach fails before a single word is written — because it's aimed at nobody in particular.

We start by mapping your actual market: the business owners, independents and professionals in your zone who match the clients you already serve best — and who are most likely to be outgrowing their current accountant. Every contact is verified before launch, so your campaigns never bounce and never guess.

A list built for your firm, your zone, your ideal mandate
Every address verified — zero bounces, zero wasted sends
One firm per zone: your prospects are never shared with a competitor
Deliverability

The best email in the world is worthless in a spam folder

Before anything is sent, we build the technical foundation most agencies skip.

Your campaigns run on dedicated sending domains — warmed up for weeks, fully authenticated (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and completely separate from your firm's real domain. Your professional address stays pristine, your reputation stays intact, and your messages land where decisions are made: the primary inbox.

Dedicated domains — your firm's email address is never exposed
94% inbox placement, monitored continuously
Volume kept deliberately low: quality sending, never blasting
Copywriting

Sober enough for your Order. Sharp enough to get replies.

Writing to a business owner about their accounting is a delicate exercise — hype kills trust, and jargon kills interest.

Every sequence is 3–4 short, human messages written in your firm's voice: exact, discreet and non-comparative, as your code of conduct requires — but built around the one thing your prospect actually cares about, their business. No templates recycled across clients. And nothing is ever sent until you've approved every word.

Compliant by design: sober, exact, non-comparative
Written in your voice, about their business — not about you
You approve every sequence before a single send
Reporting

You'll always know exactly what your investment is doing
